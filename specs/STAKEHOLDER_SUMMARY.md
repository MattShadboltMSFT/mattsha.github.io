# Jays Footy Stats - Stakeholder Summary

## What We're Building
A mobile-friendly AFL stat tracking app for junior players (Under 13's) to monitor individual performance throughout the season.

## Why This Matters
- **For Parents:** Easy way to track their child's development and celebrate improvements
- **For Players:** Visual motivation showing progress and achievements
- **For Coaches:** Data-driven insights into player performance

## What's Been Delivered
✅ **Complete Product Specification** - Ready for engineering team  
✅ **Requirements Documentation** - Clear feature definitions  
✅ **Data Model Design** - Structured approach to storing stats  
✅ **UI/UX Guidelines** - Mobile-first, kid-friendly design principles

## Key Features (Version 1.0)
1. **Quick Stat Entry** - Record full match stats in under 3 minutes
2. **Season Dashboard** - Visual progress tracking with charts
3. **Match History** - Review and edit past games
4. **Data Export** - Share reports as PDF/CSV
5. **Offline Mode** - Works at grounds with poor reception

## Timeline
- **Now:** Requirements complete ✅
- **Next:** Engineering planning and development
- **Target:** Ready for season start in April
- **Recommended Dev Time:** 6-8 weeks

## What Makes This App Special
- **Simple:** No manual required - just tap and count
- **Visual:** Kids love seeing their improvement in charts
- **Fast:** Built for use during games, not just after
- **Reliable:** Works offline, never loses data
- **Personal:** Focused on individual player growth

## Core Statistics Tracked
- Kicks, Handballs, Marks
- Goals, Behinds
- Tackles, Spoils, Smothers
- Interceptions
- Free kicks (for/against)
- Plus match context (opponent, position, etc.)

## What's NOT Included in v1.0
- Multiple player profiles (single player focus)
- Team-wide tracking
- Social sharing
- Video/photo uploads
- Cloud sync (local storage only)

*These features can be added in future versions based on user feedback*

## Technical Approach
- **Platform:** Progressive Web App (works on all devices)
- **Primary Use:** Mobile phone at games
- **Storage:** Local with export capability
- **Design:** High contrast for outdoor visibility
